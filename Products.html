Re-usable Pads

Heaven's Gait provides sustainable, affordable, and comfortable menstrual solutions for all women. Our fabric sanitary pads offer a reliable, eco-friendly alternative to disposable products. Specifically designed for normal flow, they are crafted with 100% pure cotton to ensure comfort and ease of use.
